Grey Fuse
Interdisciplinary
Academic Group Project
Julia Melo & Sofia Oliveira
2023
The theme "Art, Design and Media in response to the Climate Crisis" proposed a challenge, to explore the essence of creative practice and how it can be an active active contribution to an urgent awareness movement. The development of the project required interdisciplinary collaboration, involving different areas of specialisation within the field of design. In addition, it is is necessary for designers to engage with the community, to be able to identify, investigate and present a suitable solution to the needs identified.
