Academic Project
2023
An independent publishing house challenged me to create an editorial project on the theme/concept of theme/concept of "Memory". The aim was to develop an editorial project in different formats. The process involves a phase of analysing and investigating the the theme, including research into similar or relevant projects, the creation of mental, visual and verbal maps, as well as defining the target audience. I had to elaborate my own concept/proposal and gather all the necessary content to develop the Editorial Project, which can be materialised in any physical format that reflects the proposed concept.
