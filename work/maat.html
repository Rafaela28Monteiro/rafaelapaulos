This project consisted of formulating the visual and graphic image of a new cultural programme for the Museum of Art, Architecture and Technology (MAAT) in Lisbon. This programme, called MAAT TRANSVERSAL, is a fictitious monthly event that will will begin in March 2020 and will be organised by Maat.
